  1. $\delta$ 函数（求解常微分方程问题）
    已知：$\dfrac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}t} [p(x)\dfrac{\mathrm{d}y(x)}{\mathrm{d}t} ]+q(x)y(x)=0$ 有两个线性无关的解 $y_{1},y_{2}$ 。用Green函数求解下面微分方程：解：第一步，写出方程对应的格林函数问题
    原初值问题所对应的格林函数问题为 $$
    \begin{cases}
    \dfrac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}t} [p(x)\dfrac{\mathrm{d}g(x;\xi)}{\mathrm{d}t} ]+q(x)g(x;\xi)=\delta(x-\xi),\quad a<x;\xi<b \\
    g(a;\xi)=0,\quad y(b;\xi)=0
    \end{cases}\begin{cases}
    c_{3}(\xi)y_{1}(\xi)+c_{4}(\xi)y_{2}(\xi)=0 \\
    c_{3}(\xi)y_{1}^{\prime} (\xi)+c_{4}(\xi)y_{2}^{\prime} (\xi)=\dfrac{1}{p(\xi)}
    \end{cases}W_{1}=\left|\begin{array}{c}
    y_{1}(\xi) & y_{2}(\xi) \\
    y_{1}^{\prime} (\xi) & y_{2}^{\prime} (\xi)
    \end{array}
    \right|\neq 0g(x;\xi)=\begin{cases}
    0&t<\xi \\
    -\dfrac{y_{2}(\xi)y_{1}(x)}{p(\xi)W_{1}}+\dfrac{y_{1}(\xi)y_{2}(x)}{p(\xi)W_{1}}&x>\xi
    \end{cases}=\dfrac{-y_{2}(\xi)y_{1}(x)+y_{1}(\xi)y_{2}(x)}{p(\xi)W_{1}}\eta(x-\xi)g(x;\xi)=c_{5}(\xi)y_{1}(x)+c_{6}(\xi)y_{2}(x)+\dfrac{-y_{2}(\xi)y_{1}(x)+y_{1}(\xi)y_{2}(x)}{p(\xi)W_{1}}\eta(x-\xi)\begin{cases}
    c_{5}(\xi)y_{1}(a)+c_{6}(\xi)y_{2}(a)=0 \\
    c_{5}(\xi)y_{1}(b)+c_{6}(\xi)y_{2}(b)+\dfrac{-y_{2}(\xi)y_{1}(b)+y_{1}(\xi)y_{2}(b)}{p(\xi)W_{1}}=0
    \end{cases}W_{2}=\left|\begin{array}{c}
    y_{1}(a) & y_{2}(a) \\
    y_{1}(b) & y_{2}(b)
    \end{array}
    \right|\neq 0g(x;\xi)=\dfrac{-y_{2}(\xi)y_{1}(b)+y_{1}(\xi)y_{2}(b)}{p(\xi)W_{1}W_{2}} (y_{2}(a)y_{1}(x)-y_{1}(a)y_{2}(x))\begin{cases}
    \dfrac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}t} [p(x)\dfrac{\mathrm{d}g(x;x^{\prime} )}{\mathrm{d}t} ]+q(x)g(x;x^{\prime} )=\delta(x-t^{\prime} ),\quad a<x;x^{\prime} <b \\
    g(a;x^{\prime} )=0,\quad y(b;x^{\prime} )=0
    \end{cases}g(\xi;x^{\prime} )-g(t^{\prime} ;\xi)=[p(x)(g(x;\xi)\dfrac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}t} [p(x)\dfrac{\mathrm{d}g(x;x^{\prime} )}{\mathrm{d}t} ]-g(x;x^{\prime} )\dfrac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}t} [p(x)\dfrac{\mathrm{d}g(x;\xi)}{\mathrm{d}t} ])]_{x=a}^{x=b}=0y(x)=\int _{a}^{b}g(x;\xi)f(\xi)\mathrm{d}\xi+\{p(\xi)[y(\xi) \dfrac{\mathrm{d}g(x;\xi)}{\mathrm{d\xi}}-g(x;\xi) \dfrac{\mathrm{d}y(\xi)}{d\xi}]\}_{\xi=a}^{\xi=b}
    =\int _{a}^{b}g(x;\xi)f(\xi)\mathrm{d}\xi+\{p(\xi)[y(\xi) \dfrac{\mathrm{d}g(x;\xi)}{\mathrm{d\xi}}\}_{\xi=a}^{\xi=b}
    $$
